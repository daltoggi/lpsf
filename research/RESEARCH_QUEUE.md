# Research Queue for LPSF

Research must not be mere summarization. Every source should produce one of:

```text
- operator idea
- architecture decision
- eval method
- risk/limitation
- rejection reason
```

## Priority 1 — Direct Architecture Foundations

### Hopfield Networks / Attention as Associative Memory

Question:

```text
Can attention/attractor dynamics support the semantic gravity module?
```

Extract:

- attractor scoring;
- energy landscape interpretation;
- retrieval-as-convergence.

### Dynamic Field Theory

Question:

```text
How can cognition, memory, attention, and decision be represented as field dynamics?
```

Extract:

- field update equations;
- working memory analogs;
- stability/activation concepts.

### Differentiable Plasticity / Fast Weights

Question:

```text
How can experience change future behavior without full retraining?
```

Extract:

- plastic connection rules;
- Hebbian-style updates;
- short-vs-long timescales.

### RAG / kNN-LM / RETRO / Memorizing Transformer

Question:

```text
Where do external memory systems stop, and where does LPSF begin?
```

Extract:

- baseline designs;
- limitations;
- comparison criteria.

## Research Note Requirements

Each research note must include:

```text
- exact title
- authors
- year
- source URL/DOI
- read status
- claims directly supported
- claims inferred for LPSF
- what to use
- what not to overclaim
- operator extraction
- eval implications
```
