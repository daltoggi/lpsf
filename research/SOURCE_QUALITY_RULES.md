# Source Quality Rules

## Preferred Sources

Use in this order:

1. Peer-reviewed papers.
2. arXiv papers with strong author/institution credibility.
3. Official documentation.
4. Books from reputable academic publishers.
5. Technical blog posts by authors of the system/paper.
6. Secondary summaries only for orientation, not final claims.

## Claims Need Support

For each claim, record source type:

```text
- direct source claim
- implementation inference
- analogy
- speculation
- rejected overclaim
```

## Forbidden Research Behaviors

- Do not invent paper titles.
- Do not invent equations.
- Do not infer empirical results from theory papers.
- Do not treat metaphors as proof.
- Do not claim LPSF is validated because related fields exist.
